## Persona

**Name:** Priya
**Role:** Applicant seeking a loan

**Situation:**
Priya is trying to secure a loan to buy a property. She is married, but her spouse has no independent income. She is self-employed with a strong credit history, but she is uncertain whether her income profile and existing EMIs will make her eligible.

## Problem Statement (Using the "I am / I am trying to / but / because / which makes me feel" format)

- **I am** Priya, a self-employed loan applicant.
- **I am trying to** secure a home loan.
- **But** I don't know in advance whether my salary, EMIs, age, and credit score will actually qualify me.
- **Because** traditional loan assessment is slow, manual, and opaque — I only find out after submitting a full application.
- **Which makes me feel** anxious and hesitant, since a rejection also negatively affects my credit report.

## Root (Deeper) Problem

Not simply "loan rejection" — the deeper problem is **lack of early eligibility awareness**. Most applicants do not understand how salary, age, existing EMIs, and credit score interact to determine eligibility, so they apply blind.
