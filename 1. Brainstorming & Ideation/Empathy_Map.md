# Empathy Map — FinWise AI

**User Persona:** Priya, self-employed loan applicant

| Quadrant | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Says** | "Will I even qualify for this loan?" / "I don't want a rejection on my credit report." / "I wish I knew this before applying." |
| **Thinks** | "My EMIs are already high — will that count against me?" / "Is my credit score good enough?" / "Banks make this so confusing." |
| **Does** | Searches "loan eligibility calculator" online, compares multiple bank websites, asks friends/relationship managers informally, delays applying due to uncertainty. |
| **Feels** | Anxious, confused, slightly distrustful of opaque bank processes, hopeful but cautious. |

## Pain Points
- No clear, instant answer on eligibility
- Doesn't understand how EMI + salary + credit score combine into a decision
- Fear of a hard credit-report hit from a rejected application

## Gains (What Success Looks Like)
- Instant, clear eligibility signal before applying
- Understands *why* — e.g., "your EMI load is too high" — not just a yes/no
- Gets a personalized suggestion (e.g., reduce EMI burden, wait 6 months, improve score) to improve chances
