## Customer-Side Problems (Root Causes)
1. No early visibility into loan eligibility before formal application
2. Doesn't understand how salary, EMIs, age, credit score interact
3. Fear of a rejected application hurting credit score further

## Triggers (What Prompts the User to Act)
- Planning a major purchase (home, vehicle)
- Existing EMI load increasing, wants to check headroom
- Credit score changed and wants to reassess eligibility

## Emotions After the Problem Occurs
Anxiety, confusion, hesitation to apply, mistrust of opaque bank processes

## Existing/Available Alternatives
- Paisabazaar Loan Eligibility Calculator (static, formula-based, no personalized advisory)
- Asking a bank relationship manager directly (slow, manual)

## Behavior After Using Existing Solutions
Gets a rough number but no explanation or improvement guidance — still uncertain about *why*, and no personalized next steps.

## Our Proposed Solution
FinWise AI — instant, AI-explained eligibility check + EMI calculator + personalized financial advisory, combining structured logic (salary/EMI/credit rules) with Groq AI-generated natural-language guidance.

## Fit Statement
Our solution directly addresses the root cause (lack of early, explainable eligibility awareness) rather than the symptom (loan rejection) — validating strong problem-solution fit.
