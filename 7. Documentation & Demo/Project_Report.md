## 3. Problem Statement
Many loan applicants apply without knowing whether they'll qualify, leading to rejections, wasted time, and credit-report damage. The deeper issue is a lack of early eligibility awareness — most users don't understand how salary, EMIs, age, and credit score interact to determine outcomes.

## 4. Proposed Solution
FinWise AI gives users instant, AI-explained loan eligibility analysis, credit score evaluation, EMI calculation, and personalized financial recommendations — before they formally apply.

## 5. Social Impact
Promotes financial awareness and responsible borrowing.

## 6. Business Model
Pre-screening/customer-engagement tool for banks, NBFCs, and fintech companies — reduces manual workload and unnecessary applications.
