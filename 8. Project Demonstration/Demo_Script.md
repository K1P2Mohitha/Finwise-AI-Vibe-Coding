# Demo Script — FinWise AI (Storytelling Flow)

**Total suggested time: 6–8 minutes**

## 1. Hook / Problem (45 sec)
> "Imagine you want to buy a home. You apply for a loan, wait days for a decision, and then get rejected — and now that rejection sits on your credit report too. That's the reality for thousands of loan applicants. The problem isn't that people don't qualify — it's that they don't know *in advance* whether they will."

## 2. Introduce the Solution (30 sec)
> "That's what FinWise AI solves. It's an intelligent platform that gives you instant loan eligibility, credit score evaluation, EMI calculation, and personalized financial advice — before you ever submit a formal application."

## 3. Live Demo Walkthrough (3–4 min)
Walk through it as a story, not a feature list — use one persona (e.g., "Priya") throughout:

1. **Show the input form** — "Let's say Priya wants to check if she qualifies for a home loan. She enters her salary, age, existing EMIs, and credit score."
2. **Submit and show the eligibility result** — "Within seconds, FinWise AI tells her she's *conditionally eligible* — not just a yes/no."
3. **Show the EMI breakdown** — "It also shows exactly what her monthly EMI would look like for the loan amount she wants."
4. **Show the AI advisory** — "And here's what makes this different from a static calculator like Paisabazaar — it explains *why*, in plain language, and gives her concrete next steps: reduce her existing EMI burden, or wait a few months to improve her credit score."
5. **(Optional) Show history dashboard** — "She can also track past checks if she reassesses later."

## 4. Technical Highlights (1 min)
> "Under the hood, we used Flask for the backend, Groq AI to power the reasoning behind our advisory, and SQLite to store applicant records. We built this using a design-thinking approach — starting from empathizing with an actual applicant persona, not from the code."

## 5. Impact Close (45 sec)
> "For users, this means clarity and confidence before applying. For banks, it means fewer unnecessary applications and lower screening costs. It's a tool that helps both sides of the lending relationship — and it's built on a problem that's real, relatable, and currently underserved by static calculators."

## 6. Invite Questions
> "Happy to answer any questions — about the eligibility logic, the AI integration, or the architecture."
